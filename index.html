Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Cafe.jude collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: Username, display name, and profile image stored as base64
  • Password: Stored as a one way SHA-256 hash we never store or see your actual password
  • Donation data: Amount, message, and timestamp when you make a donation
  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or payment card details

How Data Is Stored

  • All data is stored securely on Google Firebase
  • Your session is saved locally in your browser localStorage for convenience
  • Profile images are stored as base64 strings within the database

What Is Publicly Visible

  • Your username, display name, and profile image
  • Donation amounts and messages on the Cafe List
  • Your profile URL Cafe.jude/username

What Is Private

  • Your password hashed, never exposed
  • Your internal user ID
  • Your browser session data

Data Deletion

  • You may request account deletion by contacting the admin
  • The admin can remove messages from the Cafe List at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from the database

Cookies & Local Storage

  • We use localStorage to save your login session and theme preference
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third party analytics beyond Firebase/Google Analytics
