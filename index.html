Terms of Service

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to Cafe.jude ("Buyme.cafe"). By creating an account or using our platform, you agree to the following terms.

Account Creation

  • You must choose a unique username and a secure password minimum 6 characters
  • Each person may create one account. Duplicate or spam accounts may be removed
  • You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure
  • Profile images must be appropriate and non offensive

Donations & Payments

  • All donations are voluntary and non refundable
  • Payments are processed via ABA Bank / KHQR
  • Donation amounts and messages are displayed publicly on the Cafe List
  • We do not store or process payment card information directly

Messages & Content

  • Messages left with donations are publicly visible
  • You must not post spam, offensive, hateful, or illegal content
  • The admin reserves the right to delete any message without notice
  • Repeated violations may result in account restriction

Profile Editing

  • You may edit your display name and profile image
  • A cooldown of 1 hour 30 minutes applies between edits to prevent abuse
  • Usernames cannot be changed after account creation

Platform Rules

  • Do not impersonate other users or public figures
  • Do not attempt to exploit, hack, or disrupt the platform
  • We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms
  • These terms may be updated continued use constitutes acceptance

For questions, contact us via Telegram.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Cafe.jude collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: Username, display name, and profile image stored as base64
  • Password: Stored as a one way SHA-256 hash we never store or see your actual password
  • Donation data: Amount, message, and timestamp when you make a donation
  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or payment card details

How Data Is Stored

  • All data is stored securely on Google Firebase
  • Your session is saved locally in your browser localStorage for convenience
  • Profile images are stored as base64 strings within the database

What Is Publicly Visible

  • Your username, display name, and profile image
  • Donation amounts and messages on the Cafe List
  • Your profile URL Cafe.jude/username

What Is Private

  • Your password hashed, never exposed
  • Your internal user ID
  • Your browser session data

Data Deletion

  • You may request account deletion by contacting the admin
  • The admin can remove messages from the Cafe List at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from the database

Cookies & Local Storage

  • We use localStorage to save your login session and theme preference
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third party analytics beyond Firebase/Google Analytics
